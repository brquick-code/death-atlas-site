MISSION

Respectful, accurate,
and location-focused.

Death Atlas is designed for history enthusiasts, researchers, educators, and curious explorers who want a deeper geographic understanding of historical events.

Entries are presented for educational and research purposes, with a focus on documented sources, context, and respectful storytelling.
